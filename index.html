The Problem

Documents are created everywhere.Most of them go nowhere.

Invoices, receipts, expense records, delivery dockets — generated worldwide, every day. And 80–90% of all business data generated today is unstructured — scattered, unorganised, stuffed in pockets, piled on desks, or sitting unread in an email inbox.

The Pile

Every business generates documents. Few actually manage them.

  • 2.5 trillion documents created globally each year — most never used

  • 60-73% of business data goes unused for analytics

The Cost

When reporting doesn't happen, the consequences stack up fast.

  • $20.5B bookkeeping market growing 7% / yr — SMEs make up 70% of clients

  • SMEs spend 10 hours every month on bookkeeping alone

  • $3 trillion lost yearly to decisions made without past insights (MIT Sloan)

The Opportunity

Every document contains data. Most of it is never captured.

  • 53% of SMBs don't know what insights their data could provide (AWS)

  • Companies that leverage data lift revenue by up to 20% (McKinsey)

  • Data-driven organisations are 23× more likely to acquire customers

The Shift

Captured. Organised. Reported. Instantly.

  • Every scan → structured data entry

  • Every entry → live report

  • Every report → decision-ready export

  • AI answers the questions your data has waited for

Every one of those documents contains data that, if properly captured and reported, gives business owners the insight to make better decisions, reduce costs, and stay ahead — while foreseeing future losses before they happen.
