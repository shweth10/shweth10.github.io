How it works

Three steps to done.

From paper receipt to export-ready report.

1

Pick a template.

Choose from a library of built-in reports spanning Finance, Tax, Travel, Construction, Healthcare, Ops, Professional and Personal — or add your own columns, clone a built-in template, or build one from scratch in the template editor.

2

Scan with your camera.

Photograph receipts, invoices, or forms. Template-first AI reads every field — vendor, date, amounts, tax — and drops each one into the right column.

3

Export or share.

PDF, Excel with real working formulas, CSV, or a PIN-locked web link your accountant opens in any browser. No app install required on their end.

Template-first AI

You tell it what you're building — then scan.

Generic document scanners dump raw OCR into a file and leave you to sort it. LensReport does the opposite: pick a report template first, and the AI knows exactly what to extract from every photo.

Built-in templates covering Finance & Expense, Tax & Compliance, Travel & Vehicles, Construction & Trades, Healthcare & Medical, Operations & Procurement, Professional Services and Personal — with new ones added regularly. Need something the built-ins don't cover? Clone any template and add custom columns, or build a new one from scratch in minutes — the AI follows your columns whatever you define them as.

Multi-document sessions

Scan the pile. Get the report.

One report. Twenty-five receipts. Every photo becomes a row — vendor, date, amounts, tax — editable inline, ready to export. No typing. No chasing paperwork. No desktop software.

Captured offline? Every photo queues automatically and processes the moment you're back online. Editing and exporting keep working with zero connectivity.

Smart Columns

Your spreadsheet, with the math built in.

Calculated columns export as real, working Excel formulas — not hardcoded values. Change a tax rate or markup once, and every row updates the moment the file opens.

LensReport builds GST, VAT, subtotals, running totals, row counts, and your own recurring calculations straight into the template. You set the rate once. The spreadsheet takes care of itself.

Trust & control

Built with your data in mind.

We built LensReport assuming every receipt, invoice and report you capture is sensitive. The guarantees in this section exist so your data stays yours.

Zero-retention AI

Your scans run through OpenAI under Zero Data Retention. Nothing is logged, nothing trains models, nothing leaves extraction with your content attached.

Your team, your price

One subscription covers your whole crew. Invite unlimited contributors — they scan against your shared pool, free forever. Zero per-seat upsell as you grow.

Never double-count a receipt

AI fingerprinting compares vendor, date, and total across every scan. If the same document gets captured twice — from a different angle or under different lighting — we flag it before it becomes a duplicate row.

Your data, your control

Delete any entry, any report, or your whole account in one tap. No dark patterns. No retention loopholes.

Open export, no lock-in

Leave anytime with everything intact. Export to PDF, real-formula Excel, CSV, or Word — the same formats everyone else reads. Zero proprietary traps.

Every row traces back to a receipt

Tap any row in your report to open the original scanned document. Your accountant verifies every number against the source image — no guesswork, no lost paperwork.
