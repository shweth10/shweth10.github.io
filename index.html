How it works

Three steps to done.

From paper receipt to export-ready report.

1

Pick a template.

Choose from a library of built-in reports spanning Finance, Tax, Travel, Construction, Healthcare, Ops, Professional and Personal — or add your own columns, clone a built-in template, or build one from scratch in the template editor.

2

Scan with your camera.

Photograph receipts, invoices, or forms. Template-first AI reads every field — vendor, date, amounts, tax — and drops each one into the right column.

3

Ask, export or share.

Ask the AI anything about your report, then export to PDF, Excel with real working formulas, CSV, or a PIN-locked web link your accountant opens in any browser. No app install required on their end.

Template-first AI

You tell it what you're building — then scan.

Generic document scanners dump raw OCR into a file and leave you to sort it. LensReport does the opposite: pick a report template first, and the AI knows exactly what to extract from every photo.

Built-in templates covering Finance & Expense, Tax & Compliance, Travel & Vehicles, Construction & Trades, Healthcare & Medical, Operations & Procurement, Professional Services and Personal — with new ones added regularly. Need something the built-ins don't cover? Clone any template and add custom columns, or build a new one from scratch in minutes — the AI follows your columns whatever you define them as.

AI Insights

Ask anything. Get the answer from your own data.

Open the AI button on any report — a sales log, an expense ledger, an inventory count — and an adaptive dashboard greets you on the spot: top customers, sales split by category, biggest invoice, daily trend and outliers worth a second look. No taps, no wait, no setup.

Then type a question in plain English: "how much did I sell to Acme Corp?", "which day brought the most sales?", "show me invoices flagged for follow-up". LensReport's AI reads your entries, finds the matches (loose customer names, partial spellings and typos all welcome), and answers with the actual numbers and the actual line-item detail — not a hallucination. Every answer is grounded in your data, sourced when it matters, and gated behind a "start another conversation" button so context never drifts.

Available across all paid plans. Same daily limits everywhere. Owners pay for AI on their shared reports — contributors just use it.
