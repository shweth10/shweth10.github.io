LensReport Support

Help, troubleshooting, and frequently asked questions

LensReport is a mobile receipt and invoice scanner that turns photos of business documents into export-ready Excel and PDF reports with real working formulas — no manual data entry. This page covers the most common questions and tells you how to reach us if you need help.

Quick answers: Contact us  ·  Billing & subscriptions  ·  Account & sign-in  ·  Data export & deletion  ·  App features  ·  Privacy & security

Contact Us

info@lensreport.app

We aim to acknowledge every request within 2 business days and to resolve most issues within 5 business days.

When you write in, please include:

  • The device and OS version you are using (e.g. iPhone 15, iOS 18.2 or Pixel 7, Android 14).
  • The app version number, visible at the bottom of Settings.
  • A short description of what you were trying to do and what went wrong.
  • A screenshot or screen recording if the issue is visual.

If your question is about a specific report or scan, the report’s name is enough — please do not email scanned document images, since they may contain personal or financial information. We can usually help without seeing the underlying receipts.

Billing & Subscriptions

How do I subscribe?

Open the app, tap Settings → Upgrade (or tap any locked feature), choose Individual, Family, or Enterprise, and confirm the purchase through Apple App Store or Google Play. Your subscription auto-renews at the end of each billing period and can be cancelled at any time from your store account settings.

How do I cancel my subscription?

  • iPhone / iPad: open the Settings app on your device → tap your name at the top → Subscriptions → pick LensReport → Cancel Subscription.
  • Android: open the Google Play Store app → tap your profile picture → Payments & subscriptions → Subscriptions → pick LensReport → Cancel subscription.

Cancellation stops the next renewal. You keep paid access until the end of the current billing period. Cancelling does not delete your LensReport account or your data.

How do I get a refund?

All purchases are processed by Apple or Google, not by LensReport. Refund requests must be made directly to the store you bought through:

  • Apple: visit reportaproblem.apple.com, sign in with your Apple ID, find the LensReport purchase, and tap Report a Problem.
  • Google Play: visit Google Play’s refund page and follow the steps for the LensReport purchase.

If the refund is approved, the store will release the funds back to your original payment method and we will revoke the entitlement automatically. If your refund request is denied and you believe the charge was an error, email info@lensreport.app with your store receipt and we will do our best to help.

I restored my purchases but my subscription is still locked.

In Settings → Manage Subscription, tap Restore Purchases while signed in to the same Apple ID or Google account that originally bought the subscription. If the entitlement still does not unlock after a minute, sign out and back in to LensReport and retry. If that does not resolve it, email us and include the original purchase receipt from the App Store or Google Play — we can reconcile it against our subscription provider (RevenueCat) and restore access manually.

What does the free tier include?

Five entries per calendar month (scans, manual entries, and imports combined), refreshing on the 1st of each month at 00:00 UTC. Export, custom columns, Business Profile branding, AI Insights, and team collaboration require a paid subscription.

Account & Sign-In

I can’t sign in.

Most sign-in problems fall into one of these buckets:

  • Forgot password (email sign-in): on the sign-in screen, tap Forgot password? and we will email you a reset link.
  • “Wrong account”: if you originally signed up with Google or Apple, you must sign back in with the same provider — entering the same email address under a different method will create a new, empty account.
  • Sign in with Apple shows a relay email: if you used “Hide my email”, Apple gives us a random @privaterelay.appleid.com address rather than your real one. That is fine; replies from us still reach you through Apple’s relay.
  • Account was deleted: deleted accounts cannot be recovered. You can sign up again with a new account at any time.

If you have tried the steps above and still cannot sign in, email info@lensreport.app with the email address (or sign-in provider) you used.

How do I change my email or display name?

Open Settings → Edit Profile in the app. You can change your display name there. Changing the sign-in email requires re-authenticating with the new provider; if you need help with that, email us.

How do I delete my account?

Open Settings → Delete Account. Deletion is a two-step confirmation flow and requires fresh re-authentication. Once confirmed, every report, entry, scanned image, team membership, share link, AI Insights conversation, audit log, and feedback row is removed from our systems. Deleting your LensReport account does not automatically cancel an active subscription — cancel that separately through the App Store or Google Play (see above).

Data Export & Deletion

Exporting a single report

Open the report and tap the export icon (top right). Choose PDF, Excel (.xlsx), or CSV. Excel exports preserve live formulas for calculated columns — they are real working spreadsheets, not flattened values.

Exporting everything (GDPR / data portability)

Open Settings → Export my data. We assemble a JSON bundle containing your profile, every report, every entry, quota history, team memberships, invitations, and active share links, together with short-lived download links to each scanned image. The bundle is delivered via a signed link valid for 7 days. You can request a fresh export at any time.

Why is my export missing images?

Images are stored in Firebase Cloud Storage and the bundle includes signed download URLs that expire after 7 days. If you wait longer than that to download them, request a fresh export and the URLs will be regenerated.

App Features & Troubleshooting

My scan came back with missing or wrong fields.

AI extraction is excellent but not perfect — especially on handwritten receipts, faded thermal paper, or unusual layouts. Tap any field on the row to edit it inline. The change is saved immediately and any formula-based columns (totals, taxes, etc.) recalculate live.

If a particular template consistently produces poor results on a category of document, please email us a description (no images) and we will tune the extraction prompt for that template.

I captured a photo offline. Where did it go?

Offline scans are queued on your device and visible in the report sheet with a pending badge. Once your device reconnects, the queue processes automatically and the rows fill in with extracted data. There is nothing to do manually.

How do share links work?

Owners of a paid plan can generate a public share link for any report. The link is a read-only snapshot hosted at share.lensreport.app and works without the recipient installing the app. You can require a 6-digit PIN, set the expiry (1 hour, 24 hours, 7 days, or 30 days), and revoke the link at any time from Settings → Manage Shared Reports.

If a recipient enters the PIN incorrectly 5 times in a row, the link is locked for 15 minutes to prevent brute-forcing. Wait out the lockout window and try again.

How does team collaboration work?

Team and Enterprise subscribers can generate a personal invite code (e.g. LENS-AB12CD) in Settings → Collaborate and share it with the people they want to collaborate with. The other person opens LensReport, taps Join a team, enters the code, and you receive a notification to approve or decline. Approved contributors sign in with their own free accounts and consume their own monthly entry quota. To remove a contributor, go to Settings → Collaborate, tap the member, and choose Remove from team.

What is AI Insights?

AI Insights is an optional in-app advisor available to paid subscribers. Tap the AI button on any report to ask questions in plain English (“what was my biggest expense last month?”, “am I overspending on fuel?”). Answers are grounded in your own entries, formatted in your locale and currency. Each paid user gets 30 AI questions per day. AI Insights is informational only and is not a substitute for professional advice.

The app crashed or behaves strangely.

  1. Close the app fully and reopen it.
  2. Check that you are on the latest version — older builds occasionally hit issues that have already been fixed. Update from the App Store or Google Play.
  3. If it still happens, email us with the device model, OS version, app version (visible at the bottom of Settings), and a short description of what triggers the issue.

Crash data is sent automatically via Firebase Crashlytics with no scan content attached, so even without a report from you we will usually see the crash itself. Including a description of what you were doing helps us reproduce it.

Privacy & Security

Full detail is in our Privacy Policy. The short version:

  • We do not sell your data, run advertising, or track you across other apps or websites.
  • Scanned images are processed by OpenAI under a zero-data-retention agreement and stored in your Firebase Cloud Storage bucket.
  • You can delete your entire account, export all your data, opt out of AI Insights, and revoke any share link from inside Settings.
  • EEA / UK / Swiss / California users have additional rights under GDPR and CCPA — see the Privacy Policy for how to exercise them.

Privacy-specific requests (data access, deletion, portability, restriction, objection, complaint) can be sent to info@lensreport.app. We aim to acknowledge within 5 business days and to complete within 30 days, in line with GDPR’s one-month response window.
