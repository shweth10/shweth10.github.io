Delete Your LensReport Account

How to permanently delete your LensReport account and data

LensReport (the receipt and invoice scanning app published by LensReport on the App Store and Google Play) lets you delete your account and all associated data at any time. This page explains how to request deletion, what is removed, and what — if anything — is retained and for how long.

Want a copy of your data first? Before deleting, you can export everything from Settings → Export my data in the app. Deletion is permanent and cannot be undone.

How to delete your account

Option 1 — In the app (fastest)

  1. Open LensReport and make sure you are signed in to the account you want to delete.
  2. Go to Settings → Delete Account.
  3. Confirm the two-step prompt. For your security, you will be asked to re-authenticate (sign in again) before deletion proceeds.
  4. Once confirmed, your account and data are permanently deleted from our systems.

Option 2 — By email (if you can’t open the app)

Email info@lensreport.app with the subject “Delete my account”, from the email address associated with your account (or tell us which sign-in provider — Google or Apple — you used). We will verify that you own the account and then delete it. We aim to acknowledge within 5 business days and to complete deletion within 30 days, in line with GDPR’s one-month response window — usually much sooner.

What gets deleted

When you delete your account, the following are permanently removed from our systems and cannot be recovered:

  • Your account profile — email, display name, subscription state, and onboarding industry tag
  • All of your reports, entries, and extracted fields
  • All scanned document images stored in your account
  • Your team memberships and any outstanding team invitations
  • All active share links and their read-only snapshots
  • Your AI Insights conversation history, response cache, usage records, and thumbs up/down feedback
  • Your AI Insights audit log and your entry/export quota history

What is retained, and for how long

Your subscription is not cancelled by deleting your account. Subscriptions are billed by Apple or Google, not by LensReport, so you must cancel separately to stop future charges:
  • iPhone / iPad: device Settings → your name → Subscriptions → LensReport → Cancel Subscription.
  • Android: Google Play Store → profile → Payments & subscriptions → Subscriptions → LensReport → Cancel subscription.

Apple and Google keep their own records of your purchases under their respective policies; LensReport cannot delete store-side purchase history.

For fraud-prevention, security, and legal-compliance purposes, we retain a minimal account-level audit log after deletion. This log contains no document content, scan images, or report data — only high-level security events. It is kept on the legitimate-interest basis described in our Privacy Policy.
